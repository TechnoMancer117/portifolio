Borgly

It was a social network developed by me, João Pedro Borghesi de Souza. The initial intention was to be an open chat, like a WhatsApp group. You would enter the website/app and already be in a pre-existing group, without needing to click anything. Within this group, it was possible to chat, send audio and photos (and later gifs, emojis, etc.). However, over time, I added more features, such as posting photos, videos, and texts. Each function separately. This ended up making the app more generic, similar to others, and with so many new functions I ended up straying from the initial objective, which was just an open chat. It also became difficult to manage everything alone, since I didn't know everything (I'm not a genius). As I was wasting a lot of time researching everything I needed to do, I decided to discontinue the project and focus on others I had in mind.
